Reuse Poster

One of the assignments in my 2D Fundamentals class was to design a persuasive poster. I chose to design a poster to encourage people to use reusable shopping bags instead of plastic ones. These are just a few of the many versions I came up with, and reading from left to right and top to bottom gives an idea of the process I went through in designing this. The bottom right hand design is the final version.

Path To Design Poster

In my Design: Process, Context, Systems class, I was tasked with interviewing a classmate and coming up with a visual expression of her path to design. I decided to design a series of movie posters where each one represented a person, experience, or event that led her to study design. I designed each movie poster separately and then had to figure out the best way to put them all together. Again, I'm showing several versions I went through as part of my design process.
